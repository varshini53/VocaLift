Breathing Exercises

Enhance breath control and reduce anxiety

Instructions

  • Find a comfortable seated position
  • Place one hand on chest, one on belly
  • Breathe slowly and deeply through your nose
  • Focus on expanding your diaphragm, not your chest

Exercises

  1. Box Breathing: Inhale for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4
  2. Deep Belly Breathing: Inhale deeply for 6 counts, exhale slowly for 8 counts
  3. 4-7-8 Technique: Inhale for 4, hold for 7, exhale for 8
  4. Progressive Relaxation: Tense and release each muscle group while breathing
  5. Counted Breathing: Count backwards from 10 with each exhale
