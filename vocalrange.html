Vocal Range Practice

Expand your vocal variety and expression

Instructions

  • Start with gentle humming to warm up
  • Practice different pitch levels
  • Vary your volume and tone
  • Focus on smooth transitions between ranges

Exercises

  1. Humming scales: Start low and go higher, then back down
  2. Vowel sounds: Practice A-E-I-O-U at different pitches
  3. Pitch glides: Slide smoothly from low to high pitch
  4. Volume control: Say "Hello" at different volume levels
  5. Emotional range: Say the same sentence with different emotions
  6. Resonance practice: Feel vibrations in chest, throat, and head
  7. Articulation drills: Over-pronounce consonants and vowels
