What is VocaLift?

VocaLift is your personal AI-powered public speaking coach. Whether you're preparing for a presentation, a speech, or a job interview, VocaLift helps you refine your tone, clarity, posture, and emotional delivery through powerful feedback tools.

Key Features

  • 🎙 Real-time speech analysis
  • 🧠 AI-generated feedback on tone and clarity
  • 🕴 Body language and facial expression tracking
  • 📊 Progress tracking dashboard
  • 📹 Video recording and replay

Why Use VocaLift?

With VocaLift, you get instant insights into how you sound and appear — letting you practice and perfect your delivery with confidence. Designed for students, professionals, and anyone looking to communicate better!
